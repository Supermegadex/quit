Encouragement; kind words; a struggled, somewhat creepy caresses from Alphys; and shouting from Papyrus (NYEH!) all were thrown at Flowey, and he remembered what the emotions felt like. Before Flowey could do anything, though, it was gone. Depressed, the plant cried even harder, unable to understand the sad truth of his existence.
